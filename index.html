GALAXY ENGINEER

Tutorial:

You can change the size of the sun and the planet using the sliders on the menu, but be careful as if you change the size of the sun, the strength of the gravity changes! Also, you can get a better look at the planets by adjusting the camera position using the sliders, and be sure to use the "Reset Camera" button to set the camera to neutral. Alternatively, if you hold the space-bar down you can get finer control of the camera. You can also click the "Reset Simulation" button in order to reset the camera position and the size of the Sun and PlanetClick the "?" to display this message again.
